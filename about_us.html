About us

Know about surbhi pansari

Surbhi Pansari,, though a science major was attracted to colors, cuts and silhouettes as bees are attracted to Cosmos or Dahlia stalks at an early age during her college days in Chandigarh.It was then that she realized her calling and decided to hone her levels of interest further by enrolling herself into a professional course in designing. After acquiring her degree in fashion designing, like most others, she was egged to launch her own label Soon she launched her label “SURBHI PANSARI “ & it was hugely appreciated by the clients.

The stylishly royal clothes gives a whole new definition to menswear in Kolkata and they also reinstate her design philosophy – To Dress Well Is To Dress Like An Individual.

In the words of Surbhi Pansari, designer “ We hope to bridge the existing gap between innovative designs and perfect tailoring in men’s apparel”..

While times and tastes may change, the basics of being well turned out never do; apparel proclaims the man. Customers can give their wardrobes a style makeover with a repertoire of Indo-western, Jodhpuris, waist coats, bandhgala’s , suits and shirts, along with distinctively embellished occasional and party wear. It gives you an opportunity to define their own style, a chance to break away from conventional clothing options already available. So, make your very own wardrobe statement and join the men’s premiere league in style. The designer, Surbhi Pansari has kept the style quotient just right and not gone overboard with cuts, yet adding her personal touch to the silhouettes. Her designs are a breath of fresh air and we have to say that this was much awaited. HERE nothing is by accident. Everything is by design. HERE the traditional seamlessly blends with the innovative, the formal with the informal. HERE we are driven by a bold new vision of male couture that is a little more creative, a little more unexpected and a lot more confident. HERE we believe that to dress well is to dress like an individual From party wear to occasional wear, we bring you Tuxedo, Indo-Western, Waist coats, Jowahar Jackets, Jodhpuri Coats, Shirts, Kurtas, Blazers and Suits that are a cut above.
